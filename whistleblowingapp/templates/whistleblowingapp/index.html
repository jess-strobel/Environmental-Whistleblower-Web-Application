How to Use:

Interested in submitting an environmental whistle-blower report? You're at the right place! Start by either entering anonymously (no personal data will be stored), or with your google account if you want to check the status of your report later. Then simply add a title, give a brief description, and optionally include a PDF, TXT, or JPEG file to your report. After clicking submit, your report will be processed accordingly and sent to the proper authorities.
