What are some common types of environmental crimes?
  • - Illegal disposal of hazardous waste
  • - Illegal importation of certain restricted or regulated chemicals into the United States
  • - Misrepresentations about the amount of natural resources extracted from public land
  • - False representations made to avoid paying environmental fines
  • - Violations of wildlife protection laws

What protections do I have as a whistleblower?
  • - Under the US Dept. of Labor Laws, employees are immune to retaliation (including firing, laying off, demoting, denying promotion, or reducing pay, when reporting violations that fall under any of the following:
  • - Clean Air Act (CAA)
  • - Energy Reorganization Act (ERA)
  • - Federal Water Pollution Control Act (FWPCA)
  • - Solid Waste Disposal Act (SWDA)
  • - Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
  • - Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation and Liability Act (CERCLA)
